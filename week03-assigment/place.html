About Peru

Peru, located in western South America, is a culturally rich country famous for the Andes Mountains, the Amazon rainforest, and its Incan heritage. Its premier attraction, Machu Picchu, is a 15th-century "Lost City of the Incas" located 2,430 meters (7,970 ft) above sea level. This UNESCO World Heritage site and New Seven Wonder of the World is known for its remarkable dry-stone construction, agricultural terraces, and stunning Andean cloud forest setting.
